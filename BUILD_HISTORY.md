## How to build a new bundle

1. Increment `version` in `pubspec.yaml` (format: `major.minor.patch+buildNumber`)
2. Add an entry below under a new `##` heading
3. Run: `flutter build appbundle --release`
4. Upload `build/app/outputs/bundle/release/app-release.aab` to Google Play
